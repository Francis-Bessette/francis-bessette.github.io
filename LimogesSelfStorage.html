My first venture

My parents owned a property with a nice amount of land. Since they didnt do much with it, my father encouraged me to find a way to make a profit off of it. We already had some family and friends that would park their camper and trailer on the grass behind our house. There for it was a clear place to start. When I was still in grade 6 I started drawing plans and to find ways to optimize the use of the land. Every day, I would come back home and discuss what could still be inproved and the cost of everything. It's when I was in grade 11 (2019) that we were able to put everything into motion. With a lot of help from family and friends, we put in 1/2 an acre of gravel, 8 foot tall fencing, automatic gates and video surveillance.

Fast forward to 2022, there is now 1 acre of gravel, 60 outdoor spots, 10 covered parking, 6 shipping container and 8 sheds.
